About Us

Who Am I?

Richa is a dual Master’s student in Architecture Computation and Computer Science at MIT, where her research focuses on integrating generative intelligence with domain-specific insights to develop actionable, real-world applications.

She is the Co-founder of ArchiDAO, the first Decentralised Autonomous Organisation (DAO) for the AEC industry, which leverages blockchain, computational design, and immersive technologies to bridge digital and physical realms through peer-to-peer networks.

As a Research Partner at the Experiential Technology Innovation Centre (XTIC) at IIT Madras, Richa advances immersive technologies to democratize access and impact.

With nearly five years of experience in architectural and digital practice, she is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of design, fostering equitable practices, and contributing to interdisciplinary innovation that serves diverse communities.
